A prepaid Mastercard is a reloadable payment card that allows you to load funds onto it before using it for purchases. It's not linked to a bank account and can be used wherever Mastercard is accepted.

If your card is lost or stolen, you can freeze your card instantly on Breinrock’s platform, by clicking on the Freeze Card Icon under your Physical Card Details. Additionally, contact our Customer Support Team by email: cards@breinrock.com

Yes, you can withdraw cash from ATMs that accept Mastercard. However, some ATMs may charge fees. There is also a daily/monthly withdrawal limit.
